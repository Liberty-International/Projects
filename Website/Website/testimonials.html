We'd like to introduce you to

MEDport

MEDport LLC began in 1996 with the goal of creating innovative food container products that make it easier for people to live active and healthy lifestyles while away from home. Founded on a passion for transporting fresh food to work, school and events, MEDport is best known for their brand, Fit & Fresh. Their current owners, Larry Wesson, Brian Carey and Chuck Miga, continue to make healthy eating easier with their on-the go prep, storage and travel solutions. Fit & Fresh products range from sandwich, salad and fruit containers to serving dishes, shaker cups, fashionable lunch carriers and more. All of which feature their integrated ice packs that keep food fresh for hours. You can learn more at www.fit-fresh.com.
