PO Management
We are a leader in international purchase order management. Successfully managing information about the production and movement of your goods is as important as the transportation itself. Our open information system offers a unique window to all parties in your supply chain about the status of your product from purchase order placement through delivery.

Liberty's Purchase Order Management System delivers visibility into the production and movement of your goods from purchase order placement through delivery. All parties to the supply chain have access to each transaction. Our system's unique features include:

  • Piece counts reported allowing partial shipments to be easily tracked
  • System is both event-based and exception-based
  • User-friendly software facilitates minimal training and ease of use around the globe
  • Live access to Customs Clearance status
  • SKU level tracking
  • Risk management
  • Origin Inspection and Production Control Portal

Access is controlled through firewall and DMZ protection and login id and password protection. Each party is granted access only to the data sanctioned by our client. Data can be transmitted between systems and third parties via EDI, XML, or CSV format, and is available through customized reporting at the item level, as well as the purchase order level.

  • Landed cost and financial analysis reports
  • Supply chain analysis and vendor scorecard reports
  • ASN and transit visibility to the item level
  • Transportation and Customs clearance management reports
  • Intransit reports
  • Receiving scheduling report

Our Web portal is continuously enhanced to quickly respond to regulatory changes and changes to your supply chain. We actively respond and update our systems based on feedback from our clients, vendors and employees.
