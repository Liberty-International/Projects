Pricing Philosophy   Our pricing philosophy is based on a system of simple billing with as few line items as possible. We rigorously negotiate with carriers and regularly confirm pricing through second sources for competitive advantage. Our pricing model is service oriented. We offer fair market pricing for high quality, reliable service. We maintain a history of fuel surcharges on our web site and each element of our freight costs is separately reported. This allows our clients to better understand the freight cost structure and allows us to offer landed cost reporting at the pricing component level. Surcharges are quoted “at cost” and are immediately increased AND reduced based on current carrier direct cost. We remove surcharges from base pricing to insure unfair margins are not a burden during slack or low cost fuel periods.
