Trucking & Distribution
We combine the skills and experience of logistics experts to design and manage customized domestic logistics solutions that save you time and money, while giving you more control over your supply chain. Liberty delivers our distribution, trucking and warehousing services as a seamless extension of our global logistics solutions.

Liberty delivers our distribution, trucking, and warehousing services as a seamless extension of our global logistics services. We design and manage customized supply chain solutions based on proven practices with a client centered approach.  We offer many traditional distribution products that reduce time to market, and eliminate unnecessary handling costs such as:

  • Domestic LTL and FTL trucking throughout the United States
  • Cross docking, sorting, and transloading services both at origin and at destination
  • Short and long term storage
  • Projects for both multinational, and small, local importers
  • Bar code scanning and EDI services
  • HAZMAT/DG cargo handling and export packaging and labeling

We've built our reputation on delivering easy-to-use, client-focused solutions customized to your needs. Liberty operates our business in well-established facilities that are fully sprinklered, highly secure, and fully insured.  We use a system of written standard operating procedures as well as security guidelines driven by our C-TPAT and TSA certifications to deliver quality to our clients’ supply chains.

In addition to traditional distribution services, we provide a range of specialized products.  No matter where and when your distribution needs arise, we share our local and global expertise to seamlessly manage even your most challenging distribution needs.

  • Pick and pack
  • Packaging, kitting, and point of purchase build
  • Web order fulfillment
  • Reverse logistics
  • VMI, raw material, and finished goods distribution

Here’s how Liberty’s specialized logistics expertise can help your business around the globe:

  • Origin distribution, trucking, and warehousing
  • DC bypass and delivery capabilities in Asia, Europe, and major USA hubs
  • Worldwide services tailored to meet VAT refund or deferral needs
  • Worldwide 3PL and 4PL
  • Fully licensed China warehousing and distribution including BLP and FTZ

Liberty can quickly implement any of these products or services to lessen the impact of seasonality and buying patterns. We understand that your challenges require quick, easy to implement solutions.  Our best-in-class team will keep your customers satisfied and your costs under control.
